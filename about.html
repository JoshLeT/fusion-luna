About Fusion

Fusion Nightclub was established in October 2009 (news article) in the heart of St Peter Port, Guernsey. The club is open every Friday & Saturday night 23:00 - 02:15. Fusion is the biggest nightclub in Guernsey with a whopping 460 people capacity! Since it's opening it has hosted some of the biggest events in the channel islands, DJ and performers include:

  • Calvin Harris - (news article)
  • DJ Fresh - (news article)
  • Rudimental
  • Scott Mills
  • Jaguar Skills
  • MistaJam
  • Ferry Corsten
  • Chase & Status
  • Mark Robinson (Pukka Up)

Fusion changed management in 2014 (news article) and has continued to bring the true nighclub experience to Guernsey. The new managers have also provided this experience to youths by organising under 18 events and parties. There is currently a monthly under 18s club night in association with WHACK!

About Luna

Luna Bar is run under the same management as Fusion. It's often referred to as a "pre-club bar" because of it's exclusive flavoured party shots, beer pong tables and DJs, while this is true it's not all that Luna has to offer. Luna has a lot of seating so that you can enjoy a quiet drink earlier on in the night. Recently Luna has become a very popular venue for private parties.
